Potatoes

Roasted red potatoes at their best — plain and simple. Red potatoes are tossed with olive oil, salt

and pepper, then roasted to perfection.

      3 pounds small red new potatoes, halved
      ¼ cup olive oil
      1 teaspoon salt and freshly ground black pepper
      Gather all ingredients
      Toss potatoes with oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl
      Roast potatoes in the preheated oven until tender and golden brown
